Blood Pressure Monitor
Suggestions before Measuring Blood Pressure
  • Please relax yourself physically and mentally for at least 5-10 minutes
    before measurement.
  • Avoid eating, smoking, exercising, bathing and drinking (alcohol/coffee/tea) for
    at least 20-30 minutes before measuring
    your blood pressure.
  • Stress raises blood pressure. Keep calm and relaxing during measurement.
  • Please DO NOT talk or speak during measurement.
Operation
